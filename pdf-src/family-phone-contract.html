The Family Phone Contract.

A simple agreement to sign together — whether this is their first phone or a fresh start on one they already have. Fill it in as a family, both sign it, and keep it somewhere you’ll see it. It’s not about mistrust; it’s how you stay on the same team.

01 Where the phone lives

  • It charges here overnight, outside the bedroom:
  • Phone-free times: meals, homework, and the first hour of the day.
  • Phone-free zones: bedrooms and the dinner table.

02 Apps & screen time

  • New apps get a “yes” from a parent first.
  • Screen-time limit we agree on:
  • Passcodes are shared with parents.

03 Being kind & safe

  • I’ll treat people the way I’d want to be treated — in messages, comments, and games.
  • I won’t share photos or personal info without asking.
  • If a stranger messages me, I’ll tell a parent.

04 When something goes wrong

  • If I see something scary, mean, or confusing, I can come to a parent — no punishment for telling the truth.
  • If the agreement gets broken, we repair it together. First step:

05 What we promise, too — the parents

  • We’ll live by the rules we ask of you (yes, us too — phones away at meals and bedtime).
  • We won’t snoop to shame. We’ll check in to help.
  • We’ll say yes to more freedom as you show you’re ready. This grows with you.

06 Our check-in

We’ll sit down about every 90 days to see what’s working and unlock new privileges you’ve earned. First check-in:

07 Why we’re doing this

Because you matter more than any device — and because trust, once earned, is worth keeping. We’re on the same team.
