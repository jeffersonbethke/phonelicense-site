How it works

Watch. Pass. Sign.

Three steps, repeated for six weeks. Designed to feel like Driver's Ed — structured, practical, and impossible to fake your way through.

Six modules, each 6–12 minutes. Parent and teen watch side-by-side. No lecture, no panic — just real situations and clear rules.

Micro-quizzes prove they got it. Road Tests prove they can do it — turn on Screen Time, set downtime, build a no-phone zone. Parents click "verified."
